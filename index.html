Dewatering

At Dewatering Construction Etc Limited (DCEL), we specialize in providing comprehensive, client-focused groundwater control solutions tailored to meet the unique demands of each project. Our dewatering services ensure that construction sites remain dry and safe, optimizing conditions for efficient work progress and maintaining structural integrity.

Our Expertise in Dewatering
We utilize GEHO dewatering pumps, renowned for their efficiency, reliability, and cost-effectiveness. As the sole distributor of Clasal Pumps Belgium in Nigeria and West Africa, we provide unparalleled access to top-of-the-line dewatering technology. Our fleet includes nearly 30 Wellpoint Dewatering pumps, ensuring we can handle projects of any scale and complexity.
